Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

Our application, test-content-editor, only collects data necessary to facilitate video uploads to TikTok. This includes:

  • TikTok OAuth Tokens: Access and Refresh tokens provided by TikTok after user authorization.
  • OpenID: A unique identifier to confirm the authorized account.

2. How We Use Information

Data is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your account via TikTok Login.
  • Upload and publish video content to your TikTok profile as requested by you.

3. Data Storage and Security

We do not use external databases. All authentication tokens are stored locally on the user's secure environment. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

4. User Control and Data Deletion

You may revoke our access at any time through your TikTok account settings under "Security and Login." To request manual deletion of any locally stored cache, contact: individual@orangunuts.com.

5. AI Disclosure

If AI tools are used to process video content, this app complies with TikTok’s AIGC policy by ensuring all AI-generated content is appropriately labeled during the upload process.
